Build courses faster and better. Make sailing more fun!

Sailing is all about strategy. Wind shifts, currents, weather, and other sailors all play into that, but if your course isn't set right, all other factors become insignifcant.

We think better courses make sailing more fun, and we think courses can be made better and faster. RegattaHub makes that possible.

Managing race courses is hard work, especially when the wind gets shifty. We appreciate all the volunteers on local Race Committees expecially because we often are those volunteers, but we also recognize that much can be done better.

Managing a team of volunteers can be tricky. By improving communication, RegattaHub makes setting and re-setting courses faster and more accurate.
